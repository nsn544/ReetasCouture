Our Founder

Reeta Vimal Dave

Reeta Vimal Dave, the visionary founder of Reeta's Couture, is a passionate entrepreneur driven by a deep love for fashion and a commitment to craftsmanship. With years of experience in the industry, she has transformed her vision into a brand that blends timeless elegance with modern style. Reeta's journey began in Gujarat, where she developed a keen understanding of textiles and traditional craftsmanship. Her brand, Reeta's Couture, supports local artisans, empowering communities and contributing to employment generation. Through her dedication to quality and innovation, Reeta has created a luxury fashion brand that celebrates the art of clothing and promotes sustainability, making it a choice for those who appreciate fine craftsmanship and ethical fashion.
